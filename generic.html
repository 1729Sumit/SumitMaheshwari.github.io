Hi, I am Sumit Maheshwari, from India as you might have guessed. I am a physics fanatic with inclined interest in Astrophysics. I have explored my interest during my undergad at IIT Bhilai through research projects and internships as can be seen in the projects section.

My specific interests lie in stellar dynamics and Fast Radio Bursts (FRBs). I have conducted research on compact stars such as White Dwarfs and M-Dwarfs, gaining experience in both theoretical and computational aspects of astrophysics. I have also gained experience in MHD simulation through y long term project on cloud disruption in AGN outflows.

I like to read books mainly sci-fi and biographies. In spare time I like to play cricket and badminton. I also have a liking towards hiking and long walks into the woods to clear my head and for inspirations as well.
